Gym Routine by Arthur

about

Welcome to my gym routine! My workout plan is meticulously designed to target all major muscle groups with both free weights and machines, ensuring a balanced and effective approach. The routine alternates between days focused on free weights and days dedicated to machine exercises, enhancing both strength and intensity.

legs and shoulders(day 1,4)

Day Exercise Sets x Reps
Day 1: Free Weights (Legs & Shoulders) Squats 4 sets x 8-10 reps
Romanian Deadlifts 4 sets x 8-10 reps
Lunges 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
Standing Military Press 4 sets x 8-10 reps
Arnold Press 3 sets x 8-10 reps
Lateral Raises 3 sets x 12-15 reps
Day 4: Machines (Legs & Shoulders, High Intensity) Leg Press 4 sets x 12 reps
Leg Curl Machine 4 sets x 10 reps
Leg Extension Machine 4 sets x 12 reps
Seated Shoulder Press Machine 4 sets x 10-12 reps
Cable Lateral Raises 3 sets x 12-15 reps
Smith Machine Squats 3 sets x 10-12 reps

The Legs and Shoulders Workout combines lower-body strength with upper-body stability. Key exercises include Squats for overall leg power, Romanian Deadlifts for hamstrings and glutes, and Lunges for balance. For shoulders, the Standing Military Press builds strength, while the Arnold Press and Lateral Raises target deltoid development. This routine enhances both muscle growth and stability.

Back and Arms(day 2,5)

Day Exercises Sets x Reps
Day 2: Free Weights (Back & Arms) Deadlifts 4 sets x 6-8 reps
Bent-over Rows 4 sets x 8-10 reps
Pull-ups 3 sets x max reps
Barbell Curls 4 sets x 8-10 reps
Dumbbell Hammer Curls 3 sets x 10 reps
Skull Crushers 3 sets x 10-12 reps
dumbbell overhead tricep press 3 sets x 12-15 reps
Day 5: Machines (Back & Arms, High Intensity) Lat Pulldown Machine 2 sets x max reps
Seated Row Machine 2 sets x max reps
Cable Bicep Curls 3 sets x 12-15 reps
assisted chinups 2 sets x max reps
Tricep Pushdown Machine 4 sets x 10-12 reps
Cable overhead tricep pushdown 3 sets x 15 reps

This Back & Arms workout routine focuses on building strength and muscle definition using a combination of free weights and machines. Day 2 emphasizes foundational lifts like deadlifts, rows, and pull-ups to target the back and arms, enhancing core stability and overall strength. Day 5 shifts to machine exercises for high-intensity isolation, maximizing muscle engagement while maintaining form. Together, the program offers a balanced approach to developing the upper body with both functional movements and targeted, intense training.

Chest and shoulder(day 3,6)

iDay Exercse Sets x Reps
Day 3: Free Weights (Chest & Shoulders) Flat Bench Press 4 sets x 6-8 reps
Incline Dumbbell Press 4 sets x max reps
Dumbbell Flyes 3 sets x 10-12 reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 sets x 10-12 reps
Front Raises 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3 sets x 15 reps
Day 6: Machines (Chest & Shoulders, High Intensity) Chest Press Machine 4 sets x 10-12 reps
Pec Deck Fly Machine 4 sets x 12 reps
Cable Chest Flyes 3 sets x 10-12 reps
Machine Shoulder Press 4 sets x 10 reps
cable lateral raises 4 sets x max reps
Cable Front Raises 3 sets x 12-15 reps
Smith Machine Incline Bench Press 4 sets x 8-10 reps

This Chest & Shoulders workout routine offers a comprehensive approach to developing upper body strength and aesthetics. Day 3 utilizes free weights to target chest and shoulder muscles with compound movements like the flat bench press and incline dumbbell press, complemented by isolation exercises for balanced muscle growth. On Day 6, the focus shifts to machine-based exercises for high-intensity training, enhancing muscle engagement and endurance. This combination ensures effective muscle building and improved definition in the chest and shoulders.

Notes

Rest for 120 seconds between sets for hypertrophy.

For machine days, keep the intensity high by reducing rest times to 90 seconds and focus on controlled, slow movements.

Add 10-15 minutes of warm-up (cardio and dynamic stretches) before each session.
